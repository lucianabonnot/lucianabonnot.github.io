About

With a PhD in Neuroscience and 20+ years across research and hospital-based innovation, I help healthcare and medtech teams turn brain science into decision-ready clinical evidence.

I advise organizations on how to design and evaluate neuroimaging and brain biomarkers so they are scientifically credible and fit for clinical use.

Based in France, I work internationally in English, French, and Portuguese.

  • ✔ Neuroimaging biomarker strategy — Framing, context of use, and evidence pathways.
  • ✔ Early-phase neuroimaging decisions — Feasibility, validation direction, and risk assessment.
  • ✔ Clinical and real-world imaging evidence — Decision-relevant endpoints and meaningful metrics.
