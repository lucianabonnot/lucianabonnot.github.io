About Luciana

With a PhD in Neuroscience and over 20+ years of experience in research and teaching, I specialise in bridging rigorous science with real-world healthcare solutions. From performing large-scale neuroimaging studies at King’s College London to guiding cross-functional data science teams at Hospital Israelita Albert Einstein, my career spans academia, hospitals, and innovation-driven environments.

I have a deep understanding of neurological and psychiatric conditions, as well as the scientific and operational demands of neuroradiology. From day one, I have helped brain-focused initiatives in multimodal imaging align with clinical evidence, regulations, and practical application.

Today, I translate cutting-edge research and real-world evidence into practical strategies that improve patient care, products, and teams. Healthcare organisations, med-tech startups, and education providers unlock the power of brain science — whether by designing evidence‑based projects, interpreting data, or crafting engaging learning experiences.

Fluent in English, French & Portuguese, I work globally from Île‑de‑France.

  • ✔ Scientific framing & evidence alignment — clarifying concepts, shaping questions, and identifying credible pathways before protocol design begins.
  • ✔ Early-phase project guidance — Supporting brain-focused initiatives with structured thinking and strategic input from idea to initial validation.
  • ✔ Real-World Evidence Insight — Helping define relevant endpoints, context-aware metrics, and meaningful signals in brain health.
  • ✔ Clear, credible communication — Translating complex science into strategic messages for regulatory, clinical, and stakeholder contexts.
